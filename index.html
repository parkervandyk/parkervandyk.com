About

Hey there! My name is Parker Van Dyk and I'm a software engineer and photographer based in Austin, Texas. I grew up in Dallas and went on to study Management Information Systems at the University of Alabama. My career journey took me through Atlanta and Nashville before making my way back to Texas and settling in Austin.

When I'm not working, you'll find me staying active. I'm passionate about running and occasionally cycling.

I write code and am always looking to learn new things. Together with two friends, I cofounded Apptivity Partners. Our first product, sitefast, is now live.

I'm also working on a new crypto project at paynow, and we're looking to launch soon!
